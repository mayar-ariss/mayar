Mail Drones

Hyperlocal Last-mile Distribution Networks
in London

Description

The global increase in urban population directly affects the efficiency of existing transportation systems. Demography is indeed correlated with car use. However, with technological progress, new solutions can be explored. One solution to reduce delays in transportation networks could be to implement Drone Mail delivery. "


This project aims to generate and optimise a network of hyperlocal last-mile distribution depots in London, specifically in the districts of Camden, City of London, Westminster, Kensington and Chelsea. The drones dispatch mail directly to customers, aiming at delivery times of 7 minutes or less from their original warehouse. A facility selection strategy is also outlined, which allows further expansion to take place.
