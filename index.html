Born in Ohio, USA, to a Lebanese father and a Syrian mother, Mayar was raised in Paris, France. After graduating from high school, he studied propedeutics in environmental sciences and engineering at EPFL, in Lausanne, Switzerland. Mayar is now an MEng candidate in civil and environmental engineering at Imperial College London.

With general engineering knowledge, Mayar began his research experience in the fields of econometric modelling of travel demand, transport markets pricing and evaluation, and network performance and equilibrium. He also gained expertise in nonlinear structural mechanics and computational engineering methods.

Mayar is a student member at IET, ICE, and a permanent CEM YPN member of IUCN, where he is part of the Urban and Mediterranean-type Ecosystems task forces.
